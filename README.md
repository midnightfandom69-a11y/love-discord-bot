A feature-rich Discord bot designed to streamline Server Partnerships, Invite Tracking, and Community Engagement. This bot automates the partnership process, allowing users to submit advertisements through a clean, guided interface.
Features;
• Automated Partnerships: Simplified submission flow using /partnership.
 • Collects Server Name and Advertisement text (including invite links).
  • Automatically extracts links for clean listing.

• Invite Tracking: Monitor who is joining your server and identify which members are bringing in new users.
  • Custom Embeds: All bot responses and partnership posts are styled with a pink aesthetic to maintain a consistent brand identity.

• Help Command: A comprehensive .help command to guide users through available features.

• /req or .req - Initiates the partnership submission form.
• /list or .list - Displays a list of current partners with clickable invite links.
• /invites or .invites - View your total successful invites or check another member's stats.
